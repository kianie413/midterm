Bridge to Liberal Arts Success (BLAST)

Purpose

BLAST aims to develop a strong cohort of scholars from multiple backgrounds striving for academic excellence, active citizenship, and leadership. We support, develop, and retain students who may come from an under-resourced background or be the first in their family to attend a 4-year college.

Program Breakdown

Summer

  • On-campus housing with a full meal plan
  • Two courses for tufts credit
  • College workshops for exposure to Tufts resources and leadership opportunities
  • Roundtrip travel expenses for students outside of the New England Area
  • $1000 stipiend upon completion
  • Social and community bounding events

Academic Year

  • Avidising group seminars for academic and leadership development
  • BLAST sponsored events
  • Educational Enrichment Fund (EEF) for approved finances
  • Work replacement stipend for scholars with a work-study package
  • Access to resources for study abroad, research experience, and/or internship
