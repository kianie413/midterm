Financial Resources

Attending college is all fun, until the hidden costs begin to appear. The FIRST Resource Center offers a range of resources to help students in the center reach their opportunities. We understand that while Tufts covers the basic needs, there are additional costs that can be stressful for a college student to handle by themselves. Below are a list of financial resources the FIRST Resource Center offers students who are low income, first-generation, and/or undocumented.

Academics Resources

  • Book It Forward allows you to donate and access a book for free for a whole semester! If you wish to access a book, please fill out the webform!
  • Study abroad assistance
  • Supply pantry contains all any school materials you are in need of

Food Resources

  • Swipe It Forward allows for students to donate a meal swipe to other students who are in need of a meal swipe!
  • Request meals during breaks

Health and Welness Resources

  • Health insurance for students
  • Coverage for co-pays and treatment costs
  • Coverage for students who need winter housing

Unexpected Hardship Fund

Things arise during the college experience that are unexpected. We hope that each of our students reach all their opportunities. The Unexpected Hardship Fund can be requested by students in the FIRST Center community for costs they do not have funds for. Please fill out the webform to request funding.

  • Presenting at a conference
  • Fees or equipment not covered by financial aid
  • Athletic eqipment for sports
